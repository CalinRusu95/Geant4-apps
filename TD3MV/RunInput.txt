1) Visualization off/on (0/1)
2) Scattering foil material index (1: Au; 2: Al; 0: vacuum)
3) Scattering foil thickness (*= um)
4) MPE material index (1: Mylar; 2: PE; 0: vacuum)
5) MPE thickness (*= um)
6) Particle (0: proton; 1: alpha, 2: C12+, 3: Li, 4: B)
7) Beam energy (*= MeV)
8) Beam spread (*= keV)
9) Number of layers
10) Number of events
